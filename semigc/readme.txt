Example use of LLVM's shadow stack support, in combination
with a bare-bones semi-space collector. Mostly for
educational purposes (initially my own ;-)).

Compiles against LLVM 2.6 and pre-2.7 SVN.
Note: you need to have llvm-gcc as well (see build.sh why)
